About Us

Charmed Rosalyn is a small jewelry business that focuses on creating cute custom jewelry at an affordable price. We also create cute stickers such as our bubble tea series, various types of jewelry and accessories including bracelets, necklaces, beaded rings, key chains, phone charms, earrings, etc.
